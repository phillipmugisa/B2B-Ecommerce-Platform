Services

1-      Representation

Representation of selected companies that can satisfy our customer network. Demands such as providing quality products and services at reasonable prices, respecting the rights of customers, prioritizing the positive image of customers and creating the conditions for a successful and pleasant purchase.

Based on the needs of our customers, we find the desired product and then review the facilities of the manufacturers of that product, and finally reach a consultation and cooperation agreement.

On the other hand, in order to protect the interests of producers, we set up local offices and Showrooms with the approach of creating and developing an effective and successful marketing, sales and distribution network. Using local experts with the aim of managing experienced and local expert manpower to introduce the company and products. Specialized platform, human communication network among top local decision makers, targeted advertising, diversified sales portfolio are some of our successful methods.
